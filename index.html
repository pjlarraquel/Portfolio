About Me

Frontend Developer!

Hi there! My name is Patrick Larraquel, and I am a Front-end developer with expertise in several tools and frameworks. My passion lies in using my technical skills to help clients create captivating landing pages that attract and engage their target audience. My focus is always on creating clean, functional, and user-friendly designs that are optimized for performance and search engine visibility.
